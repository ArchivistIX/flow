Beginner Lessons:

Surfboard Selection: Choose a board that suits your skill level, size, and the type of waves you'll be riding. Beach Safety: Learn about rip currents, tides, and other essential beach safety information. Paddling Technique: Practice efficient paddling to catch waves with ease. Pop-Up: Learn the correct technique to stand up on your surfboard. Basic Turns: Master the basics of turning your surfboard in both directions.

Intermediate Lessons:

Wave Reading: Learn how to anticipate wave breaks and select the best waves to ride. Cutbacks: Practice turning back toward the breaking wave for a more dynamic ride. Duck Diving: Master the art of diving under oncoming waves to maintain momentum. Bottom Turns: Improve your bottom turns to set up for more advanced maneuvers. Top Turns: Learn how to perform sharp turns at the top of the wave to generate speed and power.

Advanced Lessons:

Aerials: Master the art of launching off the lip of the wave and landing back on your board. Tube Riding: Learn how to ride inside the barrel of the wave for an unforgettable experience. Floaters: Perfect the technique of riding over the breaking wave, maintaining speed and control. Roundhouse Cutback: Combine a powerful cutback with a re-entry to perform this stylish maneuver. Tail Slides: Learn how to slide your surfboard's tail for a fluid and dynamic ride.
