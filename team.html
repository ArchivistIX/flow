Meet our Team

Meet our passionate team of skilled surf instructors, each bringing their unique expertise and experiences to provide you with the best possible learning environment. Our diverse group consists of world travelers, big wave riders, oceanographers, and yoga practitioners, all sharing a love for the ocean and the sport of surfing. Together, we strive to create a supportive and inspiring atmosphere, empowering you to achieve your goals and embrace the surfing lifestyle. Join us and let our dedicated professionals guide you on your journey to ride the waves, connect with nature, and discover the joy of surfing.

John Smith

Role:

Head Surfing Instructor

Experience:

12 years

Short Bio:

John has been a professional surfer for over a decade and has competed in international championships. As a dedicated mentor, he focuses on helping students develop their individual surfing styles and guides them towards achieving their goals in the sport.

Emily Johnson

Role:

Surfing Instructor & Yoga Teacher

Experience:

8 years

Short Bio:

Emily has explored surf spots around the world, absorbing various surfing techniques and cultures. She is also a certified yoga instructor and incorporates mindfulness and balance into her surfing lessons, teaching students the importance of the mind-body connection.

Mark Adams

Role:

Surfing Instructor & Ocean Safety Specialist

Experience:

10 years

Short Bio:

Mark holds a degree in oceanography and is passionate about marine conservation. As a surfing instructor, he educates students about ocean safety, environmental awareness, and responsible surfing practices, ensuring they have a well-rounded understanding of the sport.

Lily Thompson

Role:

Longboard Surfing Instructor

Experience:

7 years

Short Bio:

Lily specializes in longboard surfing and has competed in several longboard championships. Her teaching approach focuses on the art of "hanging ten" and "noseriding," helping students master the graceful and classic style of longboarding.

Noah Anderson

Role:

Big Wave Surfing Instructor

Experience:

9 years

Short Bio:

Noah is an adrenaline junkie who thrives on riding big waves. He has participated in big wave competitions worldwide and is experienced in tow-in surfing. As an instructor, he shares his knowledge of big wave surfing techniques and safety measures, preparing students to take on the most challenging swells.

As you get to know our team members, you'll discover the depth of knowledge and passion that each instructor brings to our surfing community.
Our team members' diverse backgrounds and experiences are the backbone of our success, ensuring that you receive top-notch coaching tailored to your unique needs.
Together, we are dedicated to fostering a love for surfing, building lasting connections, and creating unforgettable memories.
We invite you to join us in experiencing the exhilarating world of surfing and becoming part of our ever-growing, vibrant community.
Surf's up, and we can't wait to share this incredible journey with you!
